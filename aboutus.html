Hundred Hills Tours

Hundred Hills Tours is a travel and tour agency based in Kigali City, Rwanda. Hundred Hills Tours caters to both group and individual clients. Since our humble beginnings, we have assisted local and foreign tourists in organizing their travel itinerary, hotel, and accommodation, travel documents such as ground transportation. We cater to numerous corporate clients, tourists, and other travel enthusiasts to bring them closer to well-loved domestic and international destinations that will not only educate them but will also build lasting memories embodied by our travel services and tours. As your personalized travel planners, we aim to bring you comfortable, satisfactory, affordable vacation packages based on your requirements, and we offer complete local and international ground arrangements at very competitive prices.
